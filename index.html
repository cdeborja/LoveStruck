Love Struck

Welcome to Love Struck

You control the final two students in school who don't believe in love. If either of them get struck by a heart or a love letter, it's game over!

Note: You can destroy love letters with spitballs and earn an additional 10 points, but the hearts won't be affected!

Dodge the incoming hearts by using the "A" and "D" keys to control the left student and the "left" and "right" keys to control the right student, shoot spitballs with "W" and "UP" keys respectively.

How long can you last in the love filled school halls without getting Love Struck!
